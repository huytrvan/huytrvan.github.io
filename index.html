It is predicted that remote work will become increasingly important in the coming decades, based on the trends of companies and employees in the last 3 years. Unfortunately, the benefits of remote work, as of now, could only be applied to mostly white-collar jobs.

This means certain mission-critical professions that need physical presence such as on-site engineers and medical staffs are lacking the ability to work remotely. Sometimes, this could expose them to potential health risks, an example is the case for medical professions during the COVID-19 pandemic globally.

At Beemo Robotics, we aim to close this gap by providing humanoid remote-work robots to serve these particular usecases. We believe it would greatly improve safety for employees and increase flexibility as well convenience for companies due to the fact that jobs are not bounded by geography.

Our mission

To make the safest, most reliable and beneficial automation technologies; and to make them accessible for mankind.
